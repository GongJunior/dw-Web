{%block title %}What is Diceware{% endblock %}

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

Diceware is a system used to generate pasphrases. The strength of Diceware lies in the idea that a group of randomly rolled standard words is very inefficient for a computer to guess. The general idea of the process is as follows:

  1. Acquire a standard dice and list.
  2. Roll the die 5 times, recording each roll in order.
  3. Select a word from the list corresponding to the rolls in step 2
  4. Repeat steps 2 & 3 until you're satisfied with the number of words

Obviously with pen and paper this would be a very time consuming process to go through every time you need a password, but would offer true randomness in password generation. While apps like this cannot truly replicate the randomness found in the real world, their purpose is to try to strike a balance between convenience and security.
